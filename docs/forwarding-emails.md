# Forwarding Emails to HubDuck

## Your duck's email address

Every duck has a unique email address in the format:

```
{duck-name}@{school-slug}.hubduck.net
```

For example: `staff@kilcolganetns.hubduck.net`

You can find this address:
- On the **duck inbox page** — in the amber banner at the top
- In **Duck Settings > General** — under "Duck Email"
- On the **admin dashboard** — on each duck card

## How to forward

### From Gmail
1. Open the email
2. Click the **Forward** button (or press `F`)
3. Paste the duck email address in the "To" field
4. Click **Send**

### From Outlook
1. Open the email
2. Click **Forward** in the ribbon
3. Enter the duck email address
4. Click **Send**

### Automatic forwarding

You can set up automatic forwarding rules in your email client to send certain emails to HubDuck automatically. For example:
- All emails from `@education.gov.ie` → forward to staff duck
- All emails from parents → forward to class duck

## What HubDuck does with your email

When an email arrives, HubDuck's AI processes it within about 60 seconds:

| What | How |
|------|-----|
| **Summary** | AI generates a 2-sentence summary of the key points |
| **Actions** | Extracts specific tasks with deadlines (e.g., "Submit return by April 11") |
| **Events** | Identifies dates, times, and locations for calendar events |
| **Importance** | Assigns HIGH, MEDIUM, or LOW based on urgency and deadlines |
| **Categories** | Tags the email (administrative, academic, student_event, etc.) |

## Tips

- **Forward the original email**, not a reply chain — HubDuck works best with clean, single emails
- **One email per forward** — don't bundle multiple emails together
- **Include attachments** — HubDuck stores them and makes them available in the inbox
