# Edit AI Summary (Admin Only)

HubDuck's AI generates a summary for every email that arrives in your duck. Most of the time the summary is accurate, but occasionally the AI misses context, gets a name wrong, or misinterprets a detail. Admins can correct these summaries directly.

---

## Why edit a summary?

The AI summary is the first thing your team sees on each email card. If it's wrong, people get the wrong picture. Common reasons to edit:

- **Wrong date or time** — the AI misread "Friday 14th" as a different date
- **Wrong name** — it attributed the email to the wrong person or organisation
- **Missing context** — it left out something important that changes the meaning
- **Misleading tone** — it made something sound optional when it's actually mandatory

Correcting the summary ensures everyone in your organisation sees accurate information.

---

## Step by step

### 1. Find the email

Open your duck inbox and locate the email with the summary you want to correct.

### 2. Click the warning triangle

On the AI summary section of the email card, look for the small **warning triangle icon** in the top-right corner of the summary box. Click it.

A dropdown appears asking "What's wrong with this summary?"

### 3. Choose "Edit summary directly"

At the top of the dropdown, click **Edit summary directly** (shown in blue text). This option is only visible to admins.

The dropdown closes and the summary text becomes an editable text area.

### 4. Make your corrections

Edit the summary text to fix inaccuracies, add missing context, or clarify the meaning. The text area lets you write as much or as little as needed.

### 5. Save

Click **Save** to update the summary. The corrected version is now shown to everyone who views this email in the duck inbox and in future digests.

Click **Cancel** if you change your mind and want to keep the original summary.

---

## Important notes

{: .warning }
> **Admin only** — Only organisation admins see the "Edit summary directly" option. Regular members can flag a summary as inaccurate (using the same warning triangle icon), but they cannot edit it.

{: .note }
> **Per-email correction** — Each summary edit applies to one specific email. If the AI consistently gets something wrong across multiple emails, each one needs to be corrected individually.

{: .note }
> **Tip:** Non-admin members can still help by clicking the warning triangle and selecting a reason (e.g., "Wrong date or time", "Missing important information"). This feedback helps admins know which summaries need attention.

---

## What members see

When a non-admin clicks the warning triangle, they see a list of feedback options instead of the edit button:

| Option | When to choose it |
|--------|------------------|
| **Wrong date or time** | The summary mentions the wrong date, time, or day |
| **Wrong person or name** | The summary attributes the email to the wrong sender or organisation |
| **Missing important information** | The summary left out something critical |
| **Completely wrong / unrelated** | The summary doesn't match the email at all |
| **Other** | Something else is off — they can describe the issue |

This feedback is recorded so the admin can review and correct the summary.
