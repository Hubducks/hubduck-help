# Digest Settings

The digest is a summary email sent to subscribers on a schedule. It includes recent emails, action items, and upcoming events.

## Configuring the digest

Go to **Duck Settings > Digest** to configure:

### Frequency

| Option | When it sends |
|--------|--------------|
| **Daily** | Every day at the configured time |
| **Weekly** | On selected days of the week |
| **Monthly** | On a specific day of the month |
| **Urgent Only** | Only when high-importance items arrive |

### Weekly schedule

Select which days to send: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday.

### Send time

Set the time of day the digest goes out (e.g., 08:00 for morning delivery).

### Content settings

Choose what to include:
- **Urgent Messages** — HIGH importance emails
- **Regular Messages** — MEDIUM and LOW importance emails
- **Calendar Events** — Extracted events with dates and locations

### Empty digest

Toggle whether to send a digest when there are no new messages. Useful for confirming "nothing new this week."

## Previewing the digest

Go to the **Preview** tab in Duck Settings to see exactly how the digest email looks with real data from your duck.

Click **Send Test to Me** to receive a test digest at your email address. The subject will be prefixed with `[Test]`.

## Digest content

Each digest email includes:
- **Header** — Duck name, date range
- **Email summaries** — One card per email with sender, subject, AI summary
- **Action sub-items** — Key tasks from each email shown underneath
- **Event sub-items** — Calendar events with dates and locations
- **Policy alerts** — Any policies requiring acknowledgment
- **Footer** — Links to open HubDuck, manage preferences, or unsubscribe
