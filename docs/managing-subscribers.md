# Managing Subscribers

Subscribers are people who have access to a duck's inbox and receive its digest emails.

## Adding subscribers

1. Go to **Duck Settings > Access**
2. Enter the email address in the "Add Subscribers" field
3. Click **Add & Send Invite**

The person will receive an invitation email with a link to join.

## Access modes

Each duck has an access mode that controls who can be added:

| Mode | Who can join |
|------|-------------|
| **Domain Only** | Only people with your school's email domain (e.g., `@kilcolganetns.com`) |
| **Invite Only** | Only people you explicitly invite |
| **Public View** | Anyone can view, but only subscribers can interact |

## Subscriber roles

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | Full control — manage settings, add/remove subscribers |
| **Member** | View emails, acknowledge, complete actions |

## Digest subscriptions

Each subscriber can be subscribed or unsubscribed from the digest:
- **Subscribed** — receives digest emails on the configured schedule
- **Unsubscribed** — can still access the inbox but doesn't receive digest emails

Toggle digest status from **Duck Settings > Access** using the Digest column.
