# Getting Started with HubDuck

## Step 1: Create your account

1. Go to [www.hubduck.net](https://www.hubduck.net) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your school email address and create a password
3. Select your role: **School Admin** (principal/secretary) or **Teacher**
4. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Set up your organisation

If you're the first person from your school:

1. You'll be taken to the **Onboarding** page
2. Enter your school name (e.g., "Kilcolgan ETNS")
3. Choose a slug — this becomes your subdomain (e.g., `kilcolganetns.hubduck.net`)
4. HubDuck creates your first duck automatically — a **STAFF** duck for whole-school communications

## Step 3: Forward your first email

Your duck has an email address like `staff@kilcolganetns.hubduck.net`. Forward any school email to it:

1. Open a school email in your email client (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
2. Click **Forward**
3. Paste the duck email address
4. Send

Within a minute, HubDuck will:
- Summarise the email using AI
- Extract action items with deadlines
- Identify calendar events
- Assign an importance level (HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW)

## Step 4: View your inbox

Go to your dashboard at `https://kilcolganetns.hubduck.net/admin/dashboard` to see:
- All forwarded emails with AI summaries
- Action items that need attention
- Calendar events extracted from emails
- Your team and subscribers
