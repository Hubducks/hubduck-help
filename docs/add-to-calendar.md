# Add School Events to Your Calendar

HubDuck automatically extracts dates and events from school emails. You can add these events to your personal Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar so they appear alongside your other appointments.

## Add a Single Event

Every event card in your duck inbox has a small calendar icon.

1. Open any email in your duck inbox
2. Look for the blue event card showing the date, time, and location
3. Click the **calendar icon** on the event card
4. Your browser downloads a `.ics` file
5. Open the file — it opens in your default calendar app
6. Click **Add** or **Save** to confirm

The event is now in your personal calendar with the correct date, time, and location.

## Subscribe to a Duck Calendar (Recommended)

Instead of adding events one at a time, you can **subscribe** to a duck's calendar. New events appear automatically as HubDuck processes emails — no manual action needed.

### How to Subscribe

1. Go to your duck's calendar page (or look for the **subscribe** link below the mini calendar)
2. Click **Subscribe**
3. A dialog appears with your calendar URL and quick actions

### Google Calendar

1. In the Subscribe dialog, click **Add to Google Calendar**
2. Google Calendar opens — click **Add calendar**
3. Done. Events appear within 24 hours.

Or manually:
1. Click **Copy** to copy the calendar URL
2. Open [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com)
3. On the left sidebar, click **+** next to "Other calendars"
4. Click **From URL**
5. Paste the URL and click **Add calendar**

For detailed instructions, see [Google's guide to subscribing to calendars](https://support.google.com/calendar/answer/37100).

### Apple Calendar (iPhone)

1. Copy the calendar URL from HubDuck
2. Go to **Settings** → **Calendar** → **Accounts**
3. Tap **Add Account** → **Other**
4. Tap **Add Subscribed Calendar**
5. Paste the URL and tap **Next** → **Save**

### Apple Calendar (Mac)

1. Copy the calendar URL
2. In Calendar: **File** → **New Calendar Subscription**
3. Paste the URL and click **Subscribe**

### Outlook (Desktop)

1. Copy the calendar URL
2. In Outlook: **Calendar** → **Add calendar** → **Subscribe from web**
3. Paste the URL and click **Import**

### Outlook (Web)

1. Copy the calendar URL
2. Go to [outlook.live.com/calendar](https://outlook.live.com/calendar)
3. Click **Add calendar** → **Subscribe from web**
4. Paste the URL, give it a name, and click **Import**

## Why Subscribe?

| | Add single event | Subscribe |
|---|---|---|
| **Effort** | Click per event | One-time setup |
| **Updates** | Manual — if the date changes, your calendar is wrong | Automatic — changes sync within 24 hours |
| **New events** | Must add each one | Appear automatically |
| **Best for** | One-off events you want to be sure about | Ongoing awareness of all school events |

## FAQ

**How often does it update?**
Google Calendar checks every 6–24 hours. Outlook and Apple Calendar check every few hours. New events from HubDuck appear within a day.

**Can I edit synced events?**
No — subscribed events are read-only. To change an event, update it in HubDuck.

**Does it cost anything?**
No. Calendar subscription is a free feature built into every calendar app.

**What if I unsubscribe?**
All synced events disappear from your calendar. Your HubDuck data is unaffected. You can re-subscribe at any time.

**Will I see events from all ducks?**
Each duck has its own calendar feed. If you're a member of multiple ducks (e.g., Staff Room + 3rd Class), subscribe to each one separately.
