# Bulk Archive Emails (Admin Only)

Need to clean up a duck inbox? Admins can select multiple emails and archive them all at once. Archived emails are removed from the inbox for all users in the organisation.

---

## Step by step

### 1. Open your duck inbox

Navigate to the duck whose emails you want to clean up. You'll see the list of email cards as usual.

### 2. Enter bulk mode

In the inbox toolbar (above the email list), click the **trash icon** button. This puts the inbox into bulk selection mode.

- The trash icon button turns red to show you're in bulk mode
- A selection toolbar appears above the email list

### 3. Select emails

Each email card now has a **checkbox** in the top-left corner. Tick the emails you want to archive.

You can also use the toolbar buttons:
- **Select All** — selects every email in the current view
- **Deselect All** — clears your selection

The toolbar shows how many emails you've selected (e.g., "3 selected").

### 4. Click Delete

Click the red **Delete (N)** button in the selection toolbar. A confirmation dialog appears:

> **Archive N emails?**
>
> This will archive the selected emails for all users in the organization. Archived emails will no longer appear in the inbox. This action cannot be undone.

### 5. Confirm

Click **Archive** to proceed. The selected emails are archived and disappear from the inbox. Bulk mode exits automatically.

---

## Important notes

{: .warning }
> **Admin only** — Only organisation admins can see and use the bulk archive button. Regular members do not have access to this feature.

{: .warning }
> **Cannot be undone** — Archived emails are removed from the inbox for everyone in the organisation. There is no way to restore them from the inbox.

{: .note }
> **Tip:** Use bulk archive to clean up test emails you sent while setting up your duck, or to remove outdated content that's no longer relevant to your team.

---

## When to use this

| Scenario | What to do |
|----------|-----------|
| Setting up a new duck | Forward a few test emails, verify they process correctly, then bulk archive them before inviting staff |
| End of school year | Archive old emails that are no longer relevant for the new term |
| Duplicate emails | If the same email was forwarded multiple times, archive the duplicates |
| Irrelevant content | Remove emails that were forwarded to the wrong duck |
