# Welcome to HubDuck Help

HubDuck is the email assistant for schools. Forward your school emails to a duck, and HubDuck will summarise them, extract actions, and keep your team on track.

## Key Concepts

| Concept | What it means |
|---------|--------------|
| **Duck** | An inbox that receives and processes school emails. Each duck has its own email address. |
| **Action** | Something extracted from an email that needs to be done — with a deadline and priority. |
| **Digest** | A summary email sent to subscribers on a schedule (daily, weekly, monthly). |
| **Policy** | An official school document that requires staff acknowledgment. |
| **Subscriber** | Someone who receives digest emails and can view the duck's inbox. |
